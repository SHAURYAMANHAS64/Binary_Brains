Women's Self Defence Techniques

Basic Safety Principles

  • Stay aware of your surroundings
  • Trust your instincts
  • Maintain confident body language
  • Keep emergency contacts readily available

Essential Self-Defence Moves

1. Palm Strike

An effective strike using the heel of your palm, targeting the nose or chin. Safer than punching and requires less training.

2. Knee Strike

A powerful close-range strike targeting the groin or thighs. Very effective when grabbed from close range.

3. Elbow Strike

A close-range defensive move that can be used to strike an attacker's face or body when grabbed.

Wrist Grab Escape
